Assuming you're already inside the Vim editor **without any pending modifications** to save:

Press the ESC key to make sure you're in normal mode, which you don't need to understand for this action. Then press colon followed by the letter **q** to get:

```bash
:q
```

After pressing the **ENTER** key, done! You'll be out of Vim.
