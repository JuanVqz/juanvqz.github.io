Remember that a few days ago I posted an article where I started [contributing](https://juanvasquez.dev/blog/primera-contribucion-en-el-repositorio-alacritty-themes/) to the **Alacritty-Themes** project? Since then, several things have happened.

> [Alacritty-Themes version 4.1.2](https://github.com/rajasegar/alacritty-themes/releases/tag/v4.1.2)

[Rajasegar Chandran](https://github.com/rajasegar), the creator of this package, invited me to be part of the project. From there, we joined efforts to solve several problems in the library.

The most important problem that was solved is that, when updating the user's **alacritty.yml** file, **ALL** the comments they had in their YAML file were deleted. This version fixes that problem.

### [I invite you to try Alacritty-Themes 🌈😍](https://github.com/rajasegar/alacritty-themes)
